CURRENTLY




MiLLYJaWN Studio is a blooming multimedia studio. Currently MiLLYJaWN Studio is in the beginning phases of creating their first web comic series and making a schedule for regular weekly content for youtube. Previously the studio had attempted Inktober and the "Healthy Sketch Life" series in an effort to guage audience reception and video formatting. In November 2020 the Studio launched the WTW (Worth the Watch) Blog, a series of reviews focusing on animated films. Moving into May 2021 there will regularly be two reviews a month that go up the 2nd and 4th Sunday of the month.

If you have suggestions or would like to make a recommendation for a movie you want us to write a piece on, you can send us an email at:

info@millyjawn.com

Going into the end of 2020 and the start of 2021 the company was pushing to build a foundation to be their central focus and lay a sturdy ground to build more avenues of art onto at a later time. Over the course of 2021 the company is expecting expansion into loveable and wholesome comics, more stationary products, and more apparel. The first web comic MiLLYJaWN Studio is producing will be completing it's writing phase by the end of February 2021. March will be the beginning of their storyboarding phase and character design. Mini comics will begin being posted on the IG ahead of the release of the comic which is scheduled currently for June 2021.

Custom Planners/Agendas will also be going out in June of 2021 as a part of the summer releases. The hope is that this company will grow to create awe inspiring and thought provoking content.
